ABOUT US

ANEW was founded in the year 1997 in order to work with young girls and women to significantly change their lives. We realised early on that women need not just training in employable skills but also the avenues to seek employment, and thus was born ANEW’s two-pronged focus on training and placements. At ANEW, we believe that educating a woman truly goes a long way in empowering a family, and each of our girls is a standing testament to this.Today we offer five courses – in home nursing, basic IT, advanced IT, driving and a special summer program. Thousands of our girls are successfully placed in the job market, and hundreds of families are being supported by the skills and training received in ANEW’s classrooms.ANEW is a reputed and award-winning NGO with its credibility consistently assured by Credibility Alliance, Give India Foundation and Guidestar India.
